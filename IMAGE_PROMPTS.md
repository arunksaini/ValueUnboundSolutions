# Image Generation Prompts

This document contains the AI prompts used to generate the placeholder images for the Value Unbound Solutions website. You can use these prompts with tools like DALL-E 3, Midjourney, or Adobe Firefly to regenerate these assets with your preferred style.

## Branding

### Logo (`logo.png`)
**Prompt**:
> A modern, minimal abstract logo for a consulting firm named 'Value Unbound Solutions'. The design should imply 'unbinding' or 'releasing' potential. Professional color palette: dark blue and teal. Vector style, flat design, white background.

## Home Page

### Hero Background (`hero-bg.png`)
**Prompt**:
> Abstract technology background for a corporate website hero section. Dark blue and teal gradient, subtle geometric patterns, network lines, and data nodes. Professional, clean, minimal, suitable for overlaying white text. High resolution, widescreen.

## Services Page

### Salesforce Optimization (`service-salesforce.png`)
**Prompt**:

### Process Automation (`service-automation.png`)
**Prompt**:

## About Page

### Our Vision (`about-vision.png`)
**Prompt**:
> Abstract corporate vision image. Team collaboration concept, silhouettes of people connecting digital nodes, or a bridge to the future. Professional, inspiring, blue and white theme.

## General Style Guidelines
When generating improved images, aim for:
- **Style**: Modern Corporate, Clean Tech, Minimalist.
- **Colors**: Deep Navy Blue (#0f4c81), Teal (#2a9d8f), White, and Light Grey.
- **Mood**: Trustworthy, Innovative, Value-focused.
